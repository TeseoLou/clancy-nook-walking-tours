Tours

Manchester is a city filled with stories, and I’m here to bring them to life. My walking tours go beyond the usual landmarks, diving into the lives of the people who shaped these streets.
Whether we’re following the struggles and triumphs of Victorian Mancunians, uncovering the true crimes that once gripped the city, or exploring the changing fortunes of a historic suburb, each tour is designed to make history feel real and immediate.
With a focus on storytelling, rich detail, and forgotten voices, I invite you to walk with me and see Manchester in a way you never have before.

A Tale of Two Cities

A Tale of Two Cities

Step into 1850s Manchester and experience the city at a crossroads. This immersive tour follows Edward and Eithne, two Victorian Mancunians from opposite ends of society, as they navigate a rapidly changing industrial world. Inspired by real lives, the tour reveal stark contrasts between the wealthy elite and the struggling poor. Explore the slums of Little Ireland, where poverty and overcrowding defined daily life. Discover how Manchester was shifting from a milling town to a global trade center. Walking in their footsteps, through their workplaces and daily haunts, we will experience the challenges and opportunities of Victorian Manchester.

True Crime in Ancoats

True Crime in Ancoats

In the late 19th century, Ancoats was a hub of hardship, crime, and unrest. Its overcrowded slums bred gangs, pickpockets, and murderers, filling Victorian newspapers with shocking tales. This tour explores the brutal conditions that fueled crime, from poverty to urban chaos. Hear shocking stories of murder on the canal, assassination by cake, and Italian romance gone wrong. Explore the riots and street fights that shaped political change, with Ancoats at the center of Manchester’s turbulent history. Visit the homes of criminals and victims alike, including Ancoats Hospital and Tame Street Workhouse, and step into the dark heart of Victorian Manchester.

Free

120 Minutes

Kilometres

Divine Wellness, M16 7EW
