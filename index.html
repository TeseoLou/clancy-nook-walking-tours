About

I’m Clancy Nook, Manchester’s favourite raconteur! Though not born here, I arrived a decade ago and instantly fell for the city and its people. While studying an Honours degree in History at the University of Manchester, I became fascinated by its mills, mansions, and medieval alleys.

But history isn’t just about buildings and dates, it’s about the people who lived, loved, and struggled within them. That’s why my tours go beyond the surface, bringing to life the real voices of the past. From the everyday routines of factory workers to tales of scandal, tragedy, and romance, I make history vivid, personal, and unforgettable.
